PRIVACY POLICY

Collection of Your Information

We collect different types of information based on your role within the application. For users, we gather your name, address, phone number, email, city, and profile photo. For drivers, we collect your name, phone number, email, date of birth, Aadhar number, driving license (DL), DL document, profile picture, address, and details about your vehicle, including the vehicle name, vehicle type, vehicle photo, load capacity, RC card, insurance, state, district, and taluk. This information helps us ensure a smooth and personalized experience for both users and drivers.

Cookies

Our web application uses cookies to improve your user experience by tracking your preferences and session details. Cookies help us ensure that you can efficiently interact with features like vehicle search, price calculations, and order history. These cookies are essential for offering a dynamic, personalized experience, such as displaying relevant vehicles and their details based on your location. While you can browse sections of the Platform without the need of sharing any information with us, however, please note we do not offer any product or service under this Platform outside India and your personal data will primarily be stored and processed in India. Security Precautions

We take your privacy and security seriously. All personal information, including passwords, is securely stored and encrypted to prevent unauthorized access. Profile pictures and other sensitive data are transmitted using secure methods. Regular security checks and updates are performed to ensure that your data remains safe throughout your interactions with our application.

Your Rights

You have the right to access, modify, or delete your personal information at any time. Our profile management feature allows you to update details such as your name, address, and profile picture directly through your account. If you wish to remove your data from our platform entirely, please contact us, and we will assist you in doing so.

Your Consent

By using our web application, you consent to the collection and use of your personal information as outlined in this privacy policy. We will not share your data with third parties unless required by law or necessary to provide you with the services requested, such as booking vehicles.

Our company is committed to providing seamless customer support. The web application allows you to book vehicles, manage profiles, view vehicle ratings, calculate prices, and more. Should you have any questions or concerns regarding your data, or need assistance with any feature, our support team is available to help.
